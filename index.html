How Taxat thinks

One algorithm, four lenses.

The Taxat engine runs the same logic used by your future HMRC caseworker – only continuously, across every client at once.

Defence

Computes a defence score and grade for each return, based on data coverage, evidence paths and rules completeness.

  • Box‑level evidence paths
  • Defence score out of 100
  • Portfolio‑wide defence banding

Risk

Flags anomalies across HMRC, ledger, bank and platform sources with explainable hints, not black‑box alerts.

  • Cross‑source comparisons
  • Delta‑based risk hints
  • Autopilot severity bands

Parity

Measures how closely your computation aligns to HMRC’s own view, so you understand where disputes are likely to land.

  • HMRC vs Taxat compute views
  • Income stream parity scoring
  • Gap narratives ready‑made for clients

Autopilot

Turns defence, risk and parity into a single ordered queue of actions, each with clear context and impact.

  • Mission‑level priority formula
  • Contextualised actions by client
  • Filing‑aware planning
